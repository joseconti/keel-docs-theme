Sync inventory

Push your stock to Basalt and reconcile differences.

On this page
  • Manual sync
  • Resolve conflicts

01Manual sync

  1. Open the panel

    Go to Basalt → Sync.

  2. Choose the scope

    The whole catalog or a single category.

  3. Run

    Click Sync now and wait for the summary.

What you should see

A summary with items sent, updated and skipped.

02Resolve conflicts

If a SKU changed on both sides, Basalt flags a conflict and keeps the most recent value.

Note

You can force the store value from the conflicts list.
